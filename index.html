About Summit


The Africa Aviation Innovation Summit is an upcoming event designed to unite leaders, innovators, policymakers, and stakeholders in the aviation sector, focusing on the theme of "Customer Obsession in Aviation" for its 2023 edition. With the African aviation industry playing a pivotal role in the continent's economy by supporting millions of jobs and substantial economic activity, the summit aims to address the evolving needs of customers in the dynamic industry, especially in the wake of the pandemic. By facilitating discussions, networking, and fostering a competitive business environment, the summit strives to enhance the aviation innovation landscape in Africa, encouraging participants to adapt to industry shifts and exploit business opportunities for mutual growth in the region.

Objectives of the summit include:

Foster aviation industry partnerships.

Facilitate knowledge exchange.

Address challenges and opportunities.

Share successful case studies.

Explore innovative solutions.

Develop customer-centric strategies.

Ald Geordin Hill-Lewis

Exec. Mayor – Cape Town

Mr Hill-Lewis is the Mayor of the City of Cape Town He was elected to Parliament in 2011 at the age of 24, at the time the youngest MP to be elected. He served as the DA’s shadow minister for Finance and shadow minister for Trade and Industry. Mr Hill-Lewis began his involvement in politics in the arena of student politics at the University of Cape Town, launching the DA’s first student branch and eventually winning student elections on that campus. He has a Master’s degree in Finance from the University of London.

Julian Kyula

CEO and Founder – EdomX

Julian Kyula is a highly sought after, accomplished and well-recognized entrepreneur and thought leader both on a Pan-African and Global scale. In 2010 he founded the MODE Group; a highly successful multi-million dollar international fintech company that went on to become a global leader in cashless microservices with a presence in over 14 African countries and 26 countries globally. Julian is now the Chairman of (amongst other companies) Beulah City Ltd. A group he founded to develop housing projects across Africa. Beulah’s unique value positioning, in an industry already saturated with developers, is providing high-end housing solutions at prices that do not discriminate against the emerging middle class and within what is considered upscale in location.

The Venue


The Africa Aviation Innovation Summit will take place at the esteemed Southern Sun Waterfront in Cape Town, South Africa. Nestled within the vibrant Waterfront precinct, this modern and luxurious venue offers a captivating backdrop of the ocean and Table Mountain. Its convenient location, sophisticated facilities, and stunning views provide an ideal setting for attendees to engage in insightful discussions, network with industry peers, and immerse themselves in the future of aviation innovation within the African continent.
